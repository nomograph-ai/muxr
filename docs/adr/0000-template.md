## Context

The forces at play: the problem, the constraints, what makes this a decision
worth recording. Write it so a reader who was not there understands *why* a
choice was needed. Stay neutral about the outcome here.

## Decision

What we decided, in the active voice ("muxr does X"). One clear position. While
the record is `Proposed` this is the proposal; when it flips to `Accepted` the
text stays put and only the Status line advances.

## Consequences

What follows from the decision -- the good, the bad, and the follow-ups.
Include non-goals and anything a future reader might otherwise re-litigate.

## Design detail (optional)

RFC-depth material for records that need it: interfaces, types, code sketches,
layering, wire formats. Omit for small decisions.

## Alternatives considered (optional)

Each option weighed and why it lost -- so a tempting-but-rejected idea, or a
later-superseded approach, is not revisited blindly.
